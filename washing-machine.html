Washing Machine

Description

Small hydroelectric power station to show the principle of renewable energy and manufacturing of complex turbine blades using 3D printing. The turbine shaft is connected to a driving gear as an input to a small gear box located under the water entrance channel to transform the energy to the motor shaft.

Tasks

  1. Drawing 2D sketch of a vortex turbine blade with the calculated blade.
  2. 3D printing the first trial of the external parts with the suitable material (Temperature Based Color-Changing Filament to check for any excessive heat from electric parts or friction â€“ Transparent filament for the water tanks)
  3. Assembling the external body of the prototype and the tubes with the suitable fasteners.
  4. Designing and 3D printing several trials of brushing mechanisms , brushes and the best speed for the brushless DC motor
  5. The last task was to assemble the mechanism to the internal part in the outer body , then connecting the pumps to the buttons to control the flow of water.
  6. The prototype process took around a month to reach this final result after a lot of trials and experiments on different mechanisms , time of pumping water in the washing spot , the level of the brush and several challenges faced through the process
